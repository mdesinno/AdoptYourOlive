Our Deepest Mission

The Solidarity Tree

There is one last project we are working on, and it is the one I care about the absolute most. This isn't marketing; this is my family's true story.

34 years ago, in 1992, my family faced an unimaginable pain. My sister left us at just 11 years old due to leukemia. I was 5. My father channeled that emptiness into something vital: he founded the A.G.A.P.E. Association, which for over three decades has supported the families and children of the pediatric onco-hematology ward at the San Giovanni Rotondo hospital.

By the end of this year, we will launch the Solidarity Adoptions: cumulative on one special tree, The Solidarity Tree, growing in symbolic value through the year. It's a 100% digital service at around €49, with 80% going directly to the Association (we only cover taxes and platform fees). The adopter gets a digital certificate, a photo of the tree, a thank-you letter from A.G.A.P.E., and a Club Card with the same benefits as our standard adopters.

"Because of this dynamic — and I know you will understand — this is the only project that involves absolutely zero discount codes and zero creator commissions. You are absolutely not obligated to talk about it, sponsor it, or pitch it to your community. Our main business remains in our other products. But if one day you simply feel like sharing this initiative, you will have my deepest gratitude."
