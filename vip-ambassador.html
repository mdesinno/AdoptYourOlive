How Your Commission Works Here

Managing large volumes means huge orders and a massive 15% commission for you. Since standard discount codes don't apply to these tailored orders, here is our clever workaround to track your sales:

Your followers will use your code to order a Sample Kit. They pay for the sample to physically touch the bottle and taste the oil. Once they confirm their final event order, we completely deduct the cost of the sample from their total, and that initial code links their final order straight to your dashboard. Our Deepest Mission

The Solidarity Tree

There is one last project we are working on, and it is the one I care about the absolute most. This isn't marketing; this is my family's true story.

34 years ago, in 1992, my family faced an unimaginable pain. My sister left us at just 11 years old due to leukemia. I was 5. My father decided to channel that devastating emptiness into something vital: he founded the A.G.A.P.E. Association. For over three decades, this association has been economically and psychologically supporting the families and children of the pediatric onco-hematology ward at the San Giovanni Rotondo hospital.

By the end of this year, we will launch the Solidarity Adoptions. Unlike our standard program, these adoptions will be cumulative on a single, special olive tree—The Solidarity Tree. It will grow in symbolic value throughout the year, representing our collective social commitment.

It will be a 100% digital service, with a fixed cost of likely €49. 80% of the proceeds will go directly to the Association (we only keep the minimum costs to cover taxes and platform fees). The adopter will digitally receive the adoption certificate, a photo of the tree, and later, a direct thank-you letter from A.G.A.P.E. Furthermore, because those who do good deserve to be rewarded, they will receive a Club Card with a valid Member ID, granting them all the benefits offered to our standard adopters.

"Because of this dynamic — and I know you will understand — this is the only project that involves absolutely zero discount codes and zero creator commissions. You are absolutely not obligated to talk about it, sponsor it, or pitch it to your community. Our main business remains in our other products. But if one day you simply feel like sharing this initiative, you will have my deepest gratitude."
